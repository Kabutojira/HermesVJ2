# Neon Rain Crossing

A low, bounded architectural orbit frames one ceremonial transit tower off-axis at a finite intersection. Sparse cyan and magenta practicals illuminate dark facades; only three irregular pavement patches carry glossy emissive reflection cues, so the road never reads as a full mirror. Rain density scales by quality and freezes for reduced motion. The pulse brightens the tower without changing the complete fallback composition. No remote assets, cube capture, local composer, or water.
