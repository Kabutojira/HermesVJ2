# Sunlit Canyon chapter brief

## Composition

A dry, monumental sandstone corridor is the single subject. Alternating cliff masses create near, middle, and haze-softened far depth bands while the open passage provides negative space and a clear scale cue. Warm rock remains distinct from the pale blue desert sky.

## Materials and light

Use rough, non-metallic procedural rock with restrained color variation. A strong late-morning directional sun creates plausible warm faces and soft quality-gated shadows; cool sky fill retains detail in the canyon shade. The ground is dry sandstone. There is no water, mirror, reflection pass, bloom, or depth of field.

## Motion and quality

Only sparse dust moves, and it stops under reduced motion. Low quality removes shadows through shared capabilities and reduces cliff detail and dust density without changing the composition. No runtime assets are required.
