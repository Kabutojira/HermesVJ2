# Trinary Orrery

## Intent

Three rotating suns hold a clockwork family of planets in shared gravity.

## Composition

Three colored stars share one rotating central rig while four planets occupy sparse ceremonial orbits. Pulse advances the orbital phase and reveals the system as a gravitational instrument.

## Interaction and constraints

- Space/Click pulse changes emissive intensity, effect scale, and the chapter-specific energy response.
- Motion is slow, bounded, and disabled under reduced motion while pulse feedback remains visible.
- Low quality reduces particles and procedural segments; no external assets or runtime fetches are used.
- Preserve the dominant subject, deep negative space, and the chapter's restrained palette.
