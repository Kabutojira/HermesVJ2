# Ringside Descent

Shot 2 of exactly five low-Saturn-orbit visuals: a sunlit ringside descent with the station as hero and a pale plasma wake.

The shared physical contract uses 1,200 km altitude, Saturn radius 60,268 km, gravitational parameter 37,931,207.8 km³/s², a 15,547.306-second (259.122-minute) orbit, 24.841 km/s orbital velocity, and an independent 12-second station spin. Procedural atmospheric bands and a storm oval texture Saturn; local stars and rings provide depth. Reduced motion freezes spin, orbit drift, stars, and plasma. Quality tiers use 48/84/132 deterministic plasma particles. No external assets or per-frame allocations are used.
