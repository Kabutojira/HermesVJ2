# Spiral Galaxy Sanctuary

## Intent

A dense galactic congregation turns patiently around one warm, radiant heart.

## Composition

Four curved arms form the dominant spiral silhouette. Three star populations vary in color and apparent size, while a thin vertical spread and sparse surrounding field establish depth without filling the negative space.

## Interaction and constraints

- Space/Click pulse brightens and subtly expands the galaxy without changing its readable silhouette.
- Rotation is slow and ceremonial; reduced motion freezes continuous rotation while preserving pulse feedback.
- Low, medium, and high quality tiers use 720, 1,320, and 2,160 stars respectively, rendered in three point-cloud draw calls.
- Star positions are deterministic and generated once per quality tier; no external assets or per-frame allocations are used.
- Preserve the dark field, off-axis perspective, restrained bloom, and warm-white/cyan/violet palette.
