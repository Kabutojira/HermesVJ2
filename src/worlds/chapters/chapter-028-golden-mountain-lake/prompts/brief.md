# Aureate Divide

The lake is the dominant surface and leads from near-shore stone occluders to a split alpine summit. A low warm sun direction matches the Water shader and local directional light. Medium/high normal motion uses the official Three.js Water addon at the renderer-provided target size; low quality and reduced motion never construct it and retain a complete rough PBR lake. One pulse creates a restrained ring. No cube capture, second reflector, remote texture, DOF, or bloom.
